What This Calculator Shows

  • Total days: exact calendar days between the two dates.
  • Weeks and remaining days: useful for project sprints and weekly planning.
  • Months and days: calendar-aware month count, handling variable month lengths.
  • Years, months, days: human-readable breakdown.
  • Business days: weekdays only (Monday–Friday), excluding weekends.

When to Use It

Project managers use it to count working days until a deadline. Travellers use it to count days until a trip. Contract reviewers use it for notice periods. Teachers use it to plan curriculums. Anywhere you need "how long from A to B", this tool gives you a fast answer.
