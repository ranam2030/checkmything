About CheckMyThing

Simple, private tools for the questions you check every day.

CheckMyThing started with a simple observation: every day, people search for the same small answers — how old am I, what's my BMI, how many days until a date, how long will this trip take. Most of the results are cluttered with pop-ups, paywalls, sign-up walls, or tools that send your private data to a server for no good reason.

We built CheckMyThing to be the opposite of that. A clean, fast, private place to get an answer and get on with your day.

Our mission

Give everyone free access to the small calculators and tools that make everyday decisions easier — without selling their data, demanding sign-ups, or wasting their time.

What makes us different

  • Client-side by default. Our calculators run entirely in your browser. Your inputs don't touch our servers.
  • No sign-up, ever. You shouldn't have to create an account to calculate your age.
  • Readable, useful results. Plain language, clear numbers, and context that helps you understand what you're looking at.
  • Designed for mobile. Most people check us from a phone — so we design for phones first.

How we stay free

CheckMyThing is supported by unobtrusive display advertising. We never place ads inside your calculator results, and we never use your inputs for ad targeting. If you'd like to support the project without ads, we're working on a simple tip jar — stay tuned.

Get in touch

Found a bug? Have a calculator idea? Want to translate the site into your language? Drop us a message — we read everything.
