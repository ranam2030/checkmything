Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

CheckMyThing ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using checkmything.com, you agree to this policy.

1. Information our calculators collect

Our calculators run entirely in your browser. The dates, numbers, or other inputs you type into a calculator are never sent to our servers and are not stored anywhere after you close the page.

2. Information we collect automatically

Like most websites, when you visit CheckMyThing our hosting provider and analytics tools may log:

  • Your IP address (anonymised)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages you visit and time spent
  • Referring website

We use this information only to understand site usage and improve the service. We do not sell it.

3. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies for essential site functions and analytics. Third-party services (like Google AdSense and Google Analytics) may set their own cookies. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We display ads served by Google AdSense to support the site. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites.

  • Google uses the DoubleClick DART cookie to serve ads based on your visits to our site and others.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Learn more about Google's ad practices in the Google Advertising Policy.

5. Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns. Analytics data is anonymised and never linked to individual calculator inputs.

6. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalised advertising
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us.

7. Children's privacy

CheckMyThing is a general-audience site and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Third-party links

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites — please review their policies separately.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be announced on our homepage.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@checkmything.com or through our contact page.
